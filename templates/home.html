**Whispers of Dawn** In the quiet embrace of morning light, Dew-kissed petals unfurl, delicate and bright. The sun tiptoes across the horizon's edge, Painting the sky with hues of saffron pledge. Birdsong weaves through leaves, a gentle refrain, As daybreak whispers secrets to the waking plain. In this tender dance of dawn and dew, Hope blooms anew, painting the world in view.
